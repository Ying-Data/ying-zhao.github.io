About

Data is everywhere.
Clarity is rare.

Most companies don't have a data problem — they have a translation problem. Numbers pile up in spreadsheets, reports get skimmed and forgotten, and decisions still end up being made on gut feeling.

I fix that gap. With Power BI dashboards, automated ETL pipelines, and a knack for asking the right questions, I turn noisy data into the kind of insight that actually changes what a team does on Monday morning.

What makes me different? I've lived on the business side — in supply chain, international trade, and finance — so I don't just build charts. I build the right charts, for the right audience, with the right story attached.
